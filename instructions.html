Step 1: Welcome to Bedwars!

Are u ready?

Follow the five steps to learn and start your victory journey in Bedwars, a highly interactive and exhilarating video game that can build your tactical skill. Simply click on the buttons in the right-bottom corner and navigate through each step. Or click on any step on the side navigation menu to freely explore!

How to enter a game?

  1. Launch Minecraft on your device.
  2. Select “Multiplayer” on the main menu.
    3. Image Source - Minecraft Title
  3. Join a server: you will need to select a public server, such as Hypixel.
  4. Enter Bedwars: once connected, you might be in the server’s lobby. Find the Bedwars portal/sign.
    5. Image Source - Joining Server
    Image Source - Bedwars Sign
  5. Select mode: there are different modes like solo, doubles, 3v3v3v3, 4v4v4v4. Choose your preferred mode.
  6. Join a match: after clicking on the sign and selecting your mode, you will be put in a lobby and wait for other players to join.

Step 2: Basic rules that you need to know

Setup

A match will have various number of teams based on the mode you chose, for each team:

  • Usually 2 members;
  • Each team starts on a separate island with a bed and a resource generator.

Objective

  • Protect your team's bed while trying to destroy other teams' beds.
  • You can keep respawning as long as your bed still exists. If your bed is destroyed, you cannot respawn again (one life left);
  • Once you destroy another team's bed, you can kill the players on that team since they cannot respawn again.
  • The last team that survives in the game wins.

Step 3: Starting the game

Spawning

At the beginning of the game, each person will spawn on an island with a bed.

Initialising resources

  • Each island contains a “resource generator” that will generate resources (e.g. iron and gold)
  • As soon as you spawn, starting collecting resources.
  • You can use them to buy equipment such as weapons, armor or defensive materials for bed protection.

Step 4: Gameplay techniques

Building Bridge/Bridging

  • Players can build bridges to connect to other islands in order to destroy others' beds.
  • This will need basic tools like wool.
  • It's important to gradually learn fast bridging techniques to quickly and efficiently reach to other teams without falling off and die.
Image Source - Bridging

Combat

  • Players engage in combat for bed protection as well as to eliminate others.
  • There are many ways to kill other team players, such as killing with weapons (see later in Upgrade & Purchase) and pushing people off bridges, etc.
Image Source - Players' combat

Step 5: Upgrade and purchase equipment

Upgrading Generators/Equipment

Using the resources collected from generators, you can upgrade your weapons or tools. Also, you can use diamonds to upgrade your generator and get resources in shorter time and better value.

Purchasing Equipment

Using the resources, you can also purchase permanent weapons or tools from the item shop on your island.

Tips to win

  • Do not just defend! If you successfully rush and eliminate other enemies and beds, you may also dominate their resource generators. Therefore, you will have more resources to buy items.
    • Image Source - tips1
  • Use resources wisely: Don't buy expensive items with resources too soon. Some items will not be respawned once you lost them.
    • Image Source - tips2
  • Practice Bridging: Bridging fast at the beginning of the game allows you to rush to enemies' bases quicker before they even start building up protection for the bed.
